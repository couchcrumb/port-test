About me

Interactive Media
Student

Hello, my name is Courtney and I am a 25 year old second year student at Confederation College. I enjoy snowboarding at our local hill in the winter and going to the gym in the warmer seasons.

Ever since I was little, I have always had an interest in video games. Being fairly active growing up, I often had to sneak into our entertainment room at late hours of the night to hook up my old Super Nintendo. There, I fell in love with The Super Mario Brothers and The Legend of Zelda. Not even able to read yet, I some how beat the greatest games Nintendo had to offer and I was desperate for more.

At 4 years old my grandmother bought me an Original Gameboy with Pokemon Blue, Yellow and Red. I played every single game religiously, going through packs and packs of batteries.

As I grew older, my parents came to the realization that as long as I was active, I could continue to play video games. When I finished high school, I was unsure of what I wanted to do. I previously graduated from Environmental Science and also Social Service Work but I still did not think I was happy. The day I graduated from Social Service Work, I thought I was completely done with school. I continued to work as a bartender and a new Playstation 4 game was being released called "The Division". I adored that game and I could not get over how they created it. The story was phenomenal and I youtubed where and how they made the game. At that moment I knew exactly that all this time, I knew what I was made to do.

Since being in the Interactive Media program for the last 2 years, I can honestly say I am so excited for what my future holds.
